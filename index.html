The CE-RISE Solution for Digital Passports ​

CE-RISE is a EU-funded project advancing circular economy in electronics through shared data, secure systems, and open sustainability tools. CE-RISE stands for Circular Economy Resource Information System.

Within the project, CE-RISE partners have developed, piloted, and assessed digital passport approaches, applications, and system components to demonstrate practical implementation and value creation.

The CE-RISE solution makes it easy to benefit from digital passport systems.

The CE-RISE solution is OPEN: fully interoperable, open source, free to use, modular, extensible, and open to community contributions.

What it is?

A practical, open solution for building and using digital passport systems.

Why it matters?

Interoperability, openness, reuse, lower adoption cost, avoid lock-in.

What you can do with it?

Model passports, run services, connect systems, extend components, reuse openly.
